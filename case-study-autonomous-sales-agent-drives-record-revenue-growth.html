How a custom AI agent transformed lead conversion and drove record breaking revenue growth.

The Challenge

Bezninja faced a bottleneck common to rapidly growing companies: lead exhaustion. With a massive influx of new prospects and inquiries, the manual sales process couldn’t keep pace. Leads were cooling off in the inbox, and the sales team was overwhelmed by the sheer volume of initial outreach, qualification, and follow ups.

Decision makers realized that potential clients were slipping through the cracks due to delayed response times. They needed a solution that could engage prospects instantly, nurture them through the funnel, and close deals without constant human intervention.

Our Approach

Croncore developed and deployed a bespoke AI Sales Agent designed to act as a tireless, high performing member of the Bezninja team. This wasn't just a chatbot; it was a fully autonomous sales engine integrated into their core communication channels.

  • Autonomous Lead Engagement: Designed to respond to new inquiries within seconds, ensuring 100% lead coverage.
  • Proactive Prospect Nurturing: The agent was trained on Bezninja’s unique value propositions to handle objections and provide tailored product info.
  • End to End Conversion: A sophisticated logic layer allowed the agent to guide prospects from the first "hello" all the way to payment and onboarding.
01
Knowledge Base Engineering
We ingested Bezninja’s entire sales history, product documentation, and successful closing scripts to ensure the agent spoke with the brand’s specific voice and authority.
02
Lead Flow Integration
Connected the AI agent directly to their lead generation sources and CRM, allowing for seamless data synchronization and instant action.
03
Persona & Logic Fine Tuning
Spent 4 weeks optimizing the agent’s "closing logic," ensuring it could identify high intent signals and push for the sale at the optimal moment.
04
Full Scale Launch
Deployed the agent across all inbound channels, providing it with the autonomy to manage the entire top and middle of the sales funnel.
