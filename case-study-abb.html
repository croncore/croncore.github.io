The Challenge

ABB, a global leader in machinery manufacturing, was grappling with fragmented operational workflows across their enterprise. Their sales, service, and manufacturing data existed in isolated silos — Salesforce CRM on one side, legacy ERP systems and Azure cloud on the other — with no unified intelligence layer connecting them.

The result: field teams spent hours manually reconciling data, service response times were slow, and leadership had limited real-time visibility into operational KPIs. ABB needed a solution that could bridge these systems and inject AI into the heart of their day-to-day processes.

Our Approach

Croncore's team began with a comprehensive discovery phase, mapping every workflow touchpoint across ABB's global operations. We identified three core integration objectives:

  • Unify Salesforce CRM with Azure AI services to create a single operational intelligence layer
  • Deploy AI-powered workflow automation to eliminate manual data reconciliation
  • Build real-time dashboards so leadership could act on live operational data
01
Discovery & Architecture Design
4-week deep dive into existing systems, data flows, and pain points. Delivered a full architecture blueprint for the AI integration layer.
02
Salesforce–Azure AI Bridge
Built a real-time bidirectional sync layer using Azure Event Hub and custom Salesforce Apex integrations, eliminating data lag entirely.
03
Intelligent Workflow Automation
Deployed 14 AI-driven workflow automations covering service ticket routing, predictive maintenance alerts, and automated compliance reporting.
04
Global Rollout & Training
Phased global rollout across 6 regions with dedicated training sessions and a 90-day hypercare support period.
