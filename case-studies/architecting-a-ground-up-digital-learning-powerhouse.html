The Challenge

A blank slate, with the world's strictest privacy bar.

A visionary EdTech startup in Quebec had a groundbreaking pedagogical concept but lacked the technical infrastructure to bring it to life.

  • 01

    Zero infrastructure: a complete, scalable learning platform needed to be built entirely from scratch.

  • 02

    Legal fortification: operating in Quebec meant adhering to some of the world's strictest data privacy mandates, including GDPR and Law 25.

  • 03

    Proprietary AI needs: a custom trained LLM tutor was required, without the security risk of third party public AI models.

  • 04

    Pilot to launch gap: without a compliant foundation in place, the startup couldn't move from pilot to a full commercial release.

Croncore's Approach

Security and AI baked into the platform's DNA.

Croncore acted as the founding technical partner, architecting a privacy first ecosystem from the first line of code.

01

Custom Full Stack LMS Development

Engineered a bespoke Learning Management System from the ground up, optimized for high concurrency with real time student tracking and educator dashboards.

02

Sovereign AI & LLM Training

Trained a private, custom LLM on the client's curriculum and hosted it in secure local environments so no student data left the protected ecosystem.

03

Compliance by Design

Integrated a unified data layer with automated encryption and right to be forgotten protocols for full Law 25 and GDPR alignment from day one.

04

Adaptive Learning Intelligence

Built a personalization engine that analyzes student performance to dynamically adjust curriculum paths.
