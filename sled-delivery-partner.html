Compliance Posture

Built to clear your prime's risk review.

We're a remote pre-bid support partner. Our work is your response, not your infrastructure. So subcontracting Croncore doesn't add delivery risk to your bid. Here's how we keep teaming with us simple and safe.

Request compliance brief
NDA & teaming-agreement ready: We sign first. Clear scope, clear roles, your IP and bid materials protected before any work begins.
You stay the data owner: We support the response; we don't take custody of your systems or controlled data. You decide what we see.
NIST 800-171 aware handling: We handle your proposal and source materials in line with 800-171 information-handling practices.
CMMC Level 2 fluent: We know the Level 2 control expectations and help you map and document them inside your response.
Section 508 / WCAG 2.1 AA: We produce accessible proposal documents that meet the accessibility mandates SLED RFPs cite.
FERPA & CJIS aware: We recognize and respect sensitive-data handling rules when K-12 or law-enforcement materials appear in a solicitation.
Remote, US-aligned hours: A dedicated remote team that works your time zone and your deadlines.
Built around your compliance team: Our process is shaped around the questions your contracts officer will ask, so we slot into your review, not around it.
