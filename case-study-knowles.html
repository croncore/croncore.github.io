Manufacturing LLM Integration

23% increase in sales team productivity with a customized LLM system

« knowles
23% productivity boost

The Challenge

Knowles, a precision manufacturing giant, faced a critical challenge: their 200+ global sales team had no AI augmentation. Reps spent hours researching product specs, manually drafting proposals, and synthesising customer data from multiple sources before every client call.

Decision-makers needed faster, more data-driven insights but had no unified intelligence layer. The lack of an intelligent pre-call prep tool meant reps were often going into meetings underprepared or dedicating far too much time to non-selling activities.

Our Approach

Croncore designed and deployed a custom LLM system deeply integrated with Knowles' internal product catalog, CRM, and historical deal data. We focused on three key components:

  • Fine-tuned LLM: Trained on Knowles' proprietary product specs and sales playbooks.
  • AI Meeting Prep Engine: An intelligent tool that generates real-time client briefings based on CRM data.
  • Automated Proposal Drafting: A feature that significantly cuts document creation time.
01
Data Audit & LLM Foundation
Catalogued 50,000+ product SKUs and 5 years of deal data to build the training corpus for the fine-tuned model.
02
Custom LLM Fine-Tuning
Fine-tuned a base LLM on Knowles-specific language, product terminology, and sales methodologies over 6 weeks.
03
CRM Integration & Prep Engine
Integrated the LLM with Salesforce to deliver AI-generated pre-call briefings surfacing deal history, risk signals, and recommended talk tracks.
04
Deployment & Sales Enablement
Rolled out to 200+ reps globally with structured enablement workshops and in-app guided tours.

Key Takeaways

  • Fine-tuning LLMs on proprietary data unlocks competitive advantage not possible with generic AI tools.
  • Sales enablement must be central to any LLM rollout — technology alone doesn't drive adoption.
  • Integrating AI into existing CRM workflows reduces friction and accelerates time-to-value.
  • Measuring productivity at the rep level enables rapid iteration and improvement.
