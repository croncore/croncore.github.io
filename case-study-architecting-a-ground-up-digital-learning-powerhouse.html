Architecting a Ground Up Digital Learning Powerhouse

QUEBEC BASED EDTECH INNOVATOR
5× Enrollment Growth

Founding technical partner building a "Privacy First" ecosystem from the ground up for a visionary Quebec EdTech startup.

The Challenge

A visionary EdTech startup in Quebec had a groundbreaking pedagogical concept but lacked the technical infrastructure to bring it to life. They faced a "blank slate" challenge with high stakes:

  • Zero Infrastructure: They needed a complete, scalable learning platform built entirely from scratch.
  • Legal Fortification: Operating in Quebec meant adhering to some of the world’s strictest data privacy mandates, including GDPR and Law 25.
  • Proprietary AI Needs: They required a custom trained LLM that could act as a tutor without the security risks associated with third party, public AI models.

Without a robust, compliant foundation, the startup couldn't move from pilot to a full commercial launch.

Our Approach

Croncore acted as the founding technical partner, architecting a "Privacy First" ecosystem. Because we built the entire stack from the first line of code, we were able to bake security and AI integration directly into the platform's DNA.

01
Custom Full Stack LMS Development
We engineered a bespoke Learning Management System (LMS) from the ground up. Unlike off the shelf solutions, this platform was optimized for high concurrency and modular growth, featuring real time student tracking and interactive educator dashboards.
02
Sovereign AI & LLM Training
We developed a private, custom LLM specifically for the client's curriculum. By training the model on proprietary educational data and hosting it within secure, local environments, we ensured that no student data ever left the protected ecosystem.
03
Compliance by Design (GDPR & Law 25)
We integrated a unified data layer with automated encryption and "right to be forgotten" protocols. This ensured 100% alignment with Quebec government laws and international GDPR standards from day one.
04
Adaptive Learning Intelligence
We built a proprietary personalization engine that analyzes student performance patterns to dynamically adjust curriculum paths, significantly improving engagement and retention.
