Fintech Data Strategy

Reduced compliance costs by 45% through automated data workflows

FinEdge
45% cost reduction

The Challenge

FinEdge, a rapidly growing fintech platform, was facing a crisis of scale: their manual compliance checks and anti-money laundering (AML) processes were not keeping up with user acquisition. A team of analysts spent 70% of their time manually reviewing documentation and cross-referencing watchlists.

This bottleneck caused delays in customer onboarding, increased the risk of regulatory fines, and inflated operational costs. They needed to pivot from a manual review process to an intelligent, automated data strategy.

Our Approach

Croncore implemented a comprehensive data strategy focused on automating the compliance workflow using machine learning and centralized data lakes. We executed this across three main pillars:

  • Centralized Data Lake: Aggregated fragmented customer data into a secure, single source of truth.
  • Automated KYC/AML Checks: Deployed ML models to instantly verify documents and flag anomalous behavior.
  • Compliance Dashboard: Built an intuitive dashboard for analysts to review only high-risk, flagged cases.
01
Data Architecture & Security Review
Conducted a full audit of existing data pipelines ensuring SOC2 compliance and designed the architecture for a centralized Azure Data Lake.
02
KYC/AML Automation
Integrated OCR and ML vision models to automate document verification, reducing manual KYC checks by 80%.
03
Anomaly Detection Engine
Trained custom models to detect suspicious transaction patterns, significantly reducing false positives compared to legacy rule-based systems.
04
Dashboard Deployment & Training
Rolled out the new compliance dashboard to the analyst team, shifting their focus from manual data entry to high-level risk assessment.
