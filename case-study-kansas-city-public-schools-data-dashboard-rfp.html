The Challenge

A 125 point selection where integration depth and live execution carried the award.

Kansas City Kansas Public Schools set out to procure a districtwide data dashboard unifying academic, operational, and financial data for 21,000 students across 45 facilities. This was a dense, gating heavy procurement where one missed pass/fail requirement meant disqualification.

  • 01

    Comprehensive 125 Point Scorecard: Half the evaluation matrix rode on the live presentation, system integration depth, and pricing, demanding equal investment in the written response and the demo room.

  • 02

    Rigorous System Integrations: ETL pipelines required across 8 distinct district databases, including Infinite Campus, BusinessPLUS, Frontline, i Ready, and Fastbridge, each with its own schema, refresh cadence, and data ownership rules.

  • 03

    Uncompromising Gating Compliance: Hard pass/fail requirements on district data ownership, daily data refreshes, OIDC/SAML single sign on, FERPA alignment, and WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility, with zero tolerance for ambiguity.

  • 04

    Compressed schedule with sudden changes: A five week response window further destabilized when Addendum No. 1 removed the pre proposal Q&A conference, eliminating the only forum for clarifying integration assumptions.

Croncore's Approach

Six disciplines, one optimized, high scoring submission.

We worked cleanly behind the prime's brand under strict NDA to translate a dense, integration heavy EdTech solicitation into a clear, compliant, on deadline response.

01

Bid Strategy & Win Themes

Deconstructed the 125 point matrix and mapped a positioning narrative around integration certainty, data equity, and a frictionless evaluator experience.

02

Data Integration Architecture Mapping

Traced every one of the eight district source systems into a documented ETL pipeline plan covering schemas, refresh cadences, and ownership boundaries.

03

Compliance Assurance & Pass/Fail Guardrails

Built a gating requirement register verifying data ownership, daily refreshes, OIDC/SAML SSO, FERPA, and WCAG 2.1 AA conformance line by line.

04

Cost Proposal & 5 Year TCO Modeling

Structured a transparent five year total cost of ownership model aligning licensing, implementation, training, and support against the district's value criteria.

05

Implementation Timeline Engineering

Engineered a phased rollout plan sequencing integrations, validation checkpoints, and staff onboarding around the district's academic calendar.

06

Presentation & Demo Scripting

Scripted the live presentation and Q&A preparation to carry the 20 point demo segment with the same rigor as the written response.
